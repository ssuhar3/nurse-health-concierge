What We Believe

The Four Pillars of
True Advocacy

Coordination

Seamless orchestration of your care between all providers, specialists, and insurance carriers.

Communication

Plain-language explanations of diagnoses, medications, and medical decisions. No jargon, no confusion — just clarity.

Collaboration

Working alongside you, your family, and your medical team so every decision is made with full information and your consent.

Compassion

Genuine, personal care for every individual. Your loved one deserves more than a system — they deserve a true champion.

The People Behind NHC

Built on Decades of Service

53 Years of Nursing Leadership

Pat Dobbins

Founder  ·  Nurse Health Concierge

Pat Dobbins has dedicated 53 years to improving lives through nursing leadership, healthcare advocacy, and community service. Her experience spans the Veterans Administration, urban and rural hospital systems, 15 years as a nurse educator in a public school district, and ownership of her own consulting and case management business.

A former Department of Health Officer, she served as administrator of the health departments for Hendry and Glades Counties following 18 years as Executive Community Health Nursing Director — championing underserved, rural, and uninsured populations throughout her entire tenure.

"The people, the values and the communities are the best in Florida — and I will forever advocate for them."

— Pat Dobbins, Founder
