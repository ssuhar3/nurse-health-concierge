Authorization for Background Investigation

I hereby authorize Nurse Health Concierge, LLC and its designated agents to conduct a comprehensive background investigation as part of the application process. This investigation may include, but is not limited to:

  • Verification of employment history and professional references
  • Verification of professional licensing and certifications

  • Sex offender registry check
  • Verification of education credentials

I certify that all information provided in this application is true and complete to the best of my knowledge. I understand that any misrepresentation or omission may be grounds for rejection of my application or termination of engagement.

Confidentiality & HIPAA Agreement

As a Health Advocate, you will access protected health information. Please review and acknowledge the following.

HIPAA Compliance & Confidentiality Agreement

As a Health Advocate engaged by Nurse Health Concierge, LLC, I understand that I may have access to Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined by the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 (HIPAA) and its implementing regulations.

I agree to the following:

  • I will maintain strict confidentiality of all patient and client information encountered during the course of my duties.
  • I will access PHI only as necessary to perform my assigned responsibilities as a Health Advocate.
  • I will not disclose, share, copy, or distribute any PHI to unauthorized individuals or entities.
  • I will follow all Nurse Health Concierge policies and procedures regarding the handling, storage, and transmission of PHI.
  • I will immediately report any suspected breach of confidentiality or security incident to Nurse Health Concierge management.
  • I will return or destroy all PHI in my possession upon termination of my engagement.
  • I understand that violation of this agreement may result in immediate termination of engagement and may subject me to civil and criminal penalties under HIPAA and applicable state laws.

Duration

This confidentiality obligation shall survive the termination of my engagement with Nurse Health Concierge and shall remain in effect indefinitely.

Independent Contractor Agreement

Review the key terms of the independent contractor engagement.

Scope of Services

Contractor agrees to provide health advocacy services as assigned by Nurse Health Concierge, including but not limited to: care coordination, appointment management, medication oversight, insurance liaison, family communication, and home safety assessments.

Compensation Structure

Compensation will be mutually agreed upon in writing prior to the commencement of services and will be paid according to the payment schedule established by Nurse Health Concierge. Contractor is responsible for all applicable taxes as an independent contractor.

Non-Solicitation of Clients

During the term of this agreement and for a period of twelve (12) months following its termination, Contractor agrees not to directly or indirectly solicit, contact, or provide competing services to any client of Nurse Health Concierge with whom Contractor had contact during their engagement.

Confidentiality

Contractor agrees to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all business operations, client information, proprietary methods, pricing structures, and trade secrets of Nurse Health Concierge both during and after the term of this agreement.

Liability Insurance

Contractor shall maintain professional liability insurance with minimum coverage of $1,000,000 per occurrence and $3,000,000 aggregate, or as otherwise required by Nurse Health Concierge. Proof of insurance must be provided prior to the commencement of services.
